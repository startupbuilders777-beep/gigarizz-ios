## TECHNICAL NOTES FOR VIDEO EDITOR

### Specs
- **Primary format:** 16:9 (1920×1080) for YouTube/Twitter
- **Instagram:** 1080×1920 (9:16) vertical cut
- **Output codec:** H.264, 15-20 Mbps
- **Frame rate:** 30fps
- **Audio:** AAC, 256kbps, -16 LUFS (loudness normalization)

### Screenshot frames needed (for App Store static frames)
The video is for social/preview. The 5 iPhone frames and 2 iPad frames go into App Store Connect directly. See: `../fastlane/SNAPFILE.md`

### Voice-over recording
Record at 48kHz/24-bit, -18 dBFS peaks, slight room reverb. Male voice, 28-35 age range, confident but not arrogant. Think: what a cool older brother would say about his app.

### Asset checklist
- [ ] Logo animation (Lottie/After Effects source) — gold particle → G logo
- [ ] "Before" selfie (stock photo or AI-generated unflattering photo)
- [ ] "After" hero photo (AI-generated dating app quality)
- [ ] Photo generation animation (Lottie or video loop)
- [ ] Tinder/Hinge/Bumble card mockups (realistic UI, not screenshots)
- [ ] Rizz Coach screen mockup
- [ ] Tier comparison cards (Free / Plus / Gold)
- [ ] End card with logo + tagline

### Music recommendations
- **Upbeat pop:** Epidemic Sound "Fresh Start" or similar
- **Alternative:** Uppbeat "Neon Lights"
- Avoid anything with lyrics about heartbreak/rejection — keep it positive/empowering
