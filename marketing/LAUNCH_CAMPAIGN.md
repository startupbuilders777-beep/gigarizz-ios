## CRISIS/CONTINGENCY PLAN

**If listing has issues (broken screenshots, wrong description, etc):**
1. PagerDuty/alert dev team immediately
2. Submit App Store update within 2 hours
3. Post Discord: "We're aware of [issue] and pushing a fix today"

**If reviews are negative (quality issues):**
1. Respond within 24 hours with support contact
2. Escalate to dev team for bug fixes
3. Pause review prompt campaign until fixed

**If downloads are flat (under 50 by Hour 6):**
1. Double down on Reddit seeding (more authentic posts)
2. Reach out to beta users directly to boost early reviews
3. Consider App Store featured pitch to Apple
