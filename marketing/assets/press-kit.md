## What is GigaRizz?

GigaRizz is a premium AI-powered dating photo studio that transforms ordinary selfies into stunning, swipe-stopping dating profile pictures. The app features 16 distinct AI models — each producing a different photographic aesthetic — along with a built-in AI dating coach that writes bios, generates conversation openers, and suggests replies.

Unlike generic AI photo apps, GigaRizz is purpose-built for dating. Every feature, from style presets (Professional Headshot, Golden Hour, Adventure, Night Out) to the photo ranking algorithm, is optimized for one thing: helping users get more matches on dating apps.

---

## The Problem We Solve

**87% of dating app users** report that photos are the single most important factor in their swiping decisions. Yet most people use poorly lit selfies, group photos where you can't tell who's who, or images that are years old. Professional photoshoots cost $200–500+ and feel awkward. AI photo apps exist, but none are designed specifically for dating.

GigaRizz bridges this gap with AI that understands what makes a dating photo work — lighting, expression, background, composition — and applies that intelligence at scale across 16 models, letting users find their most attractive look in minutes, not hours.
